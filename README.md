# Privacy---Policy
Privacy-Policy GT Retro Racing Manager
Privacy Policy
This application is provided as a free game and respects the privacy of its users.
Information Collection and Use
The app does not require users to provide any personally identifiable information and does not directly collect, store, or share personal data.
Game Data
All game progress and settings are stored locally on the user’s device.
No game data is transmitted to external servers.
Advertising
The app may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services, such as Google AdMob.
AdMob may collect and use certain data, including but not limited to:
    • Device information
    • Advertising ID
    • IP address
    • Interaction data related to ads
This data is used to display advertisements and improve advertising performance, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.
You can review Google’s Privacy Policy at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
Analytics
The app does not use analytics or tracking services such as Firebase Analytics.
Children’s Information
This app is not intended for children under the age of 13.
The developer does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
Data Security
The developer does not store personal data and therefore does not maintain user data on external servers.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Users are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
